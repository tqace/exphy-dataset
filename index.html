Abstract

The physical world can be understood at multiple levels of abstraction, ranging from observable appearances and motions to underlying causal processes. This paper introduces the EXPHY dataset, designed to facilitate the understanding of physical principles and proposes the Intuitive World Model (IWM) for discovering intrinsic concepts and physical laws.

Dataset

The EXPHY dataset includes various physical interactions: collisions, Coulomb forces, and combined events. It features fine-grained annotations, such as object velocities and inter-object forces, to rigorously evaluate models' understanding of physical principles.

Methods

The Intuitive World Model (IWM) consists of the Search Module and the Intuitive Interaction Module (IIM). These components enable the discovery of physical principles, such as Newton's and Coulomb's laws, through interpretable modeling.

Results

The experimental results show IWM's ability to accurately simulate physical interactions, as well as its alignment with fundamental physical principles.
